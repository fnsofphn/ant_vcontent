# Thuyet Minh VContent 3.0

## 1. Muc dich tai lieu

Tai lieu nay duoc soan de thuyet minh phan mem `VContent 3.0` theo huong ro rang, de hieu va chuyen nghiep, tap trung vao 2 muc tieu chinh:

- Huong dan team san xuat van hanh cong viec dung quy trinh, dung trang thai, dung trach nhiem.
- Huong dan Admin va quan ly theo doi duoc toan bo he thong, phan quyen dung, giam that thoat thong tin va tang hieu qua cong tac phoi hop.

Noi dung tai lieu khong chi mo ta tinh nang, ma con nhan manh cach cac bo phan phoi hop voi nhau trong mot quy trinh san xuat so co nhieu vai tro, nhieu dau muc cong viec va nhieu diem ban giao.

## 2. Tong quan phan mem

`VContent 3.0` la nen tang quan ly don hang, dieu phoi san xuat va theo doi workflow cho cac module:

- `SMF`: E-learning
- `VSMF`: Video hoc lieu
- `GSMF`: Gamification

Phan mem duoc thiet ke de giai quyet 4 bai toan van hanh cot loi:

1. Tiep nhan don hang va input mot cach co cau truc.
2. Chuyen giao cong viec giua cac vai tro ma khong mat thong tin.
3. Theo doi tien do, chat luong, thanh toan va ban giao tren cung mot he thong.
4. Giup Admin va quan ly co mot diem nhin chung de dieu phoi va kiem soat.

## 3. Gia tri thuc te mang lai

### Doi voi team san xuat

- Biet ro minh dang o buoc nao trong quy trinh.
- Nhan du thong tin can thiet truoc khi bat dau thuc hien.
- Co trang thai ro rang de bao cao, ban giao va yeu cau bo sung.
- Giam viec trao doi roi rac qua chat, file rieng le, nhan tin khong luu vet.

### Doi voi PM va Admin

- Nhin thay toan canh don hang, nguoi xu ly, tinh trang tung san pham.
- Xac dinh som diem nghen, muc bi tre, task qua han, hay input chua dat.
- Chuan hoa phan quyen va pham vi truy cap theo vai tro.
- Bao dam du lieu van hanh duoc luu vet va de doi soat.

### Doi voi to chuc

- Chuan hoa cach lam viec tren cung mot nen tang.
- Giam phu thuoc vao nho nho, trao doi thu cong va kinh nghiem ca nhan.
- Tang kha nang mo rong doi ngu, vi quy trinh da duoc so hoa va nhin thay ro.

## 4. Nhom nguoi dung chinh

### 4.1. Admin

Admin la nguoi quan ly he thong o muc tong the:

- Quan ly nguoi dung, role, access scope.
- Theo doi suc khoe van hanh cua he thong.
- Kiem soat cac module, quy trinh, tieu chi QC va cac man hinh quan tri.
- Ho tro PM va cac nhom xu ly khi can thong nhat quy trinh.

Admin khong nhat thiet la nguoi truc tiep san xuat tung dau viec, nhung la nguoi dam bao he thong van hanh dung logic, dung quyen va dung chuan.

### 4.2. PM / Dieu phoi san xuat

PM la trung tam dieu phoi:

- Nhan don hang
- Kiem tra input
- Chuyen vao san xuat
- Theo doi qua trinh xu ly
- Ban giao, doi soat va thanh toan

PM dong vai tro cau noi giua khach hang, doi san xuat va quan ly van hanh.

### 4.3. Team san xuat

Bao gom:

- Storyboard
- Design
- Voice
- Video editor
- Game team
- QC

Moi nhom chi can tap trung vao man hinh va cong viec cua minh, nhung van luon thay duoc trang thai lien ket voi cac buoc truoc va sau.

## 5. Nguyen tac van hanh cot loi

Phan mem duoc xay dung tren 5 nguyen tac:

### 5.1. Mot nguon su that duy nhat

Moi thong tin quan trong ve don hang, product, input, tien do, review, payment va delivery phai duoc cap nhat tren he thong. Khong xem chat, file le hay ghi chu ca nhan la nguon chinh.

### 5.2. Moi buoc deu co dieu kien vao ra ro rang

Khong buoc nao nen duoc chuyen tiep neu chua dat dieu kien. Dieu nay giup:

- tranh day viec loi ve buoc sau
- giam QC fail do thieu thong tin
- giam tranh cai khi ban giao

### 5.3. Trang thai phai co y nghia van hanh

Trang thai tren he thong khong chi de xem cho co, ma phai phan anh dung:

- dang lam
- dang cho
- can bo sung
- da hoan thanh
- da duyet
- bi chan

Neu trang thai dung, viec phoi hop moi nhanh va minh bach.

### 5.4. Ban giao phai co dau vet

Moi lan chuyen tu nguoi nay sang nguoi kia, tu buoc nay sang buoc kia, can co:

- file hoac link
- ghi chu
- trang thai
- lich su thao tac khi can

### 5.5. Phan quyen de bao ve quy trinh

Moi vai tro chi nen thay va tac dong vao nhung man hinh can thiet. Dieu nay giup:

- tranh thao tac nham
- giu quy trinh sach
- tang tinh chuyen nghiep

## 6. Cach cac bo phan cong tac voi nhau

## 6.1. Giai doan tiep nhan va chuan hoa input

Day la giai doan PM va khach hang phoi hop chat che.

Muc tieu:

- du thong tin
- dung bieu mau
- dung file
- dung pham vi

Neu input chua day du, he thong cho phep:

- danh dau muc bat buoc
- yeu cau bo sung
- xac nhan da du dieu kien de di tiep

Y nghia doi voi team san xuat:

- Khong bi giao viec trong tinh trang mo ho.
- Giam phai lam di lam lai do thieu dau vao.

Y nghia doi voi Admin va PM:

- Kiem soat duoc chat luong intake.
- Xac dinh ro don nao du dieu kien launch, don nao dang bi chan.

## 6.2. Giai doan san xuat theo workflow

Sau khi qua intake, tung product di qua cac buoc xu ly chuyen mon.

Vi du:

- `SMF-01 -> SMF-02 -> SMF-03 ...`
- `VSMF-01 -> VSMF-02 -> VSMF-03 ...`
- `GSMF-01 -> GSMF-02 -> GSMF-03 ...`

Tai moi buoc, team san xuat can:

- nhan viec
- cap nhat tien do
- nop file hoac ket qua
- ghi chu cac diem can luu y
- chuyen trang thai dung luc

Admin va PM co the:

- theo doi tien do
- nhin thay diem tac
- xac dinh buoc nao tre
- xac dinh san pham nao dang cho phe duyet

## 6.3. Giai doan QC va phe duyet

QC khong chi la buoc kiem tra loi. QC la diem bao dam chat luong va bao dam san pham dat chuan truoc khi ban giao.

He thong can giup:

- luu tieu chi review
- phan biet pass / fail / changes requested
- ghi lai nhan xet cu the
- tra ve buoc truoc khi can

Gia tri lon nhat cua phan nay la giup doi ngu trao doi tren cung mot ngon ngu van hanh, thay vi danh gia cam tinh.

## 6.4. Giai doan ban giao va thanh toan

Sau khi hoan tat san xuat:

- PM kiem tra dau ra
- chuan bi ban giao
- ghi nhan nghiem thu
- theo doi payment request
- doi soat thanh toan

Day la giai doan de noi thong giua san xuat va tai chinh van hanh.

Neu khong co he thong, thong tin rat de bi dut doan giua:

- file da giao
- san pham nao da nghiem thu
- dot thanh toan nao da gui
- ai da xac nhan

`VContent 3.0` giup gom toan bo dau vet nay vao mot luong xuyen suot.

## 7. Vai tro cua Admin trong viec nang cao hieu qua cong tac

Admin khong chi la nguoi "quan ly he thong", ma la nguoi tao nen ky luat van hanh.

Admin can tap trung vao 6 nhiem vu:

### 7.1. Chuan hoa nguoi dung va role

- tao dung tai khoan
- gan dung role
- gan dung access scope
- dam bao moi nguoi thay dung man hinh can dung

### 7.2. Theo doi suc khoe quy trinh

Admin can nhin duoc:

- buoc nao hay bi tac
- role nao dang qua tai
- module nao tre deadline nhieu
- khu vuc nao hay phai bo sung input

### 7.3. Chuan hoa cach cap nhat trang thai

Neu team cap nhat tuy y, dashboard se mat gia tri. Admin can thong nhat:

- khi nao dung "submitted"
- khi nao dung "approved"
- khi nao dung "changes requested"
- khi nao duoc xac nhan di tiep

### 7.4. Bao dam du lieu day du va sach

Du lieu sach la nen tang de:

- bao cao
- ra quyet dinh
- doi soat
- cai tien quy trinh

### 7.5. Ho tro PM xu ly diem nghen

Admin khong can lao vao tung task, nhung can thay som va ho tro:

- can bo sung nguoi
- can doi uu tien
- can chinh role
- can mo khoa thao tac quan tri

### 7.6. Tao van hoa cong tac minh bach

Phan mem tot chi phat huy het gia tri khi doi ngu co van hoa:

- cap nhat dung
- giao tiep ro
- ban giao co dau vet
- khong lam viec ngoai luong khi khong can thiet

## 8. Cach team san xuat cong tac hieu qua tren phan mem

De cong tac tot, team san xuat nen thong nhat 8 thoi quen:

1. Chi bat dau khi input da ro va du dieu kien.
2. Moi lan nop bai deu kem ghi chu ngan gon, dung trong tam.
3. Cap nhat trang thai ngay khi cong viec thay doi.
4. Neu bi chan, phai ghi ro ly do bi chan.
5. Neu yeu cau bo sung, phai noi ro can bo sung cai gi.
6. Khong dung chat ben ngoai lam noi luu thong tin chinh.
7. Kiem tra buoc truoc va buoc sau de hieu boi canh cong viec.
8. Ton trong role va pham vi thao tac cua nhau.

## 9. Lo i ich cong tac noi bo khi ap dung dung

Khi phan mem duoc van hanh dung, to chuc se dat duoc:

- giao tiep ngan hon nhung ro hon
- it that lac thong tin hon
- it phai hoi lai hon
- giam lam lai do sai quy trinh
- PM dieu phoi de hon
- Admin kiem soat de hon
- team san xuat tap trung chuyen mon hon

Noi cach khac, phan mem khong chi giup "quan ly cong viec", ma giup ca to chuc chuyen tu cach lam viec thu cong sang cach van hanh co he thong.

## 10. De xuat cach trinh bay khi thuyet minh cho noi bo

Neu dung tai lieu nay de thuyet trinh cho team, nen theo thu tu:

1. Tai sao can mot he thong chung.
2. VContent 3.0 giai quyet nhung van de nao.
3. Moi nhom nguoi dung duoc loi gi.
4. Workflow van hanh tu intake den payment.
5. Admin va team san xuat can phoi hop theo nguyen tac nao.
6. Ky vong ve ky luat cap nhat va ban giao.

## 11. Ket luan

`VContent 3.0` la cong cu de chuan hoa van hanh san xuat noi dung so trong moi truong co nhieu role, nhieu buoc va nhieu diem ban giao.

Gia tri lon nhat cua he thong khong nam o cho "co nhieu man hinh", ma nam o cho:

- moi vai tro biet minh can lam gi
- moi buoc co dieu kien ro rang
- moi lan ban giao deu co dau vet
- PM va Admin co du thong tin de dieu phoi va ra quyet dinh

Khi team san xuat va Admin cung su dung he thong mot cach ky luat, phan mem se tro thanh mot trung tam cong tac chung: ro rang hon, nhanh hon, minh bach hon va chuyen nghiep hon.
